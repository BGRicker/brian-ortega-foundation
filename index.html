I am Brian Ortega. I am a professional MMA fighter; a Gracie Jiu-Jitsu black belt

I created this foundation in an effort to help others. I want to inspire people of all ages to Be the Change. Be the change you’d like to see in this world. My foundation is a nonprofit organization completely ran by unpaid volunteers. 100% of proceeds raised through this foundation are donated back to our community and beyond.

"I want to help people out. That’s my main thing. I love kids. I love helping kids out. I always say I’m not the perfect person, but I have the perfect heart when it comes to helping people out.”

Cause List

Brian plans on continuing his work with the Gracie Family conducting various seminars which also include the bully proof seminar for kids. He would like to raise funds to support kids who could not afford to attend.

Brian would like to participate in a variety of activities which could also include helping the homeless. – haven’t decided what that looks like yet.

Throughout the year we will hold fundraising events where supporter can sign up to help or attend. Events such as formal dinners with entertainment, celebrity appearances, meet and greets, silent auctions etc.
